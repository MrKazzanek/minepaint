Weather System

The weather system applies a visual effect to the canvas in the form of rain or snow. This effect is displayed as a texture beneath all layers, which means it does not cover the drawing elements—instead, elements (such as blocks) on the layers appear above the weather effect.

To enable rain or snow on the canvas, go to the Weather panel and select one of the three available options:

  • Clear – disables the weather effect
  • Rain – enables the rain effect
  • Snow – enables the snow effect
Warning

Only one weather effect can be enabled on a single canvas at a time.
