What can you do with MinePaint?

MinePaint isn’t limited to one purpose – you can use it for things like:

World Panoramas

With a wide selection of blocks, you can create detailed Minecraft-style panoramas of existing biomes or entirely custom landscapes.

House Floor Design

With a vast selection of blocks, you can create the perfect floor pattern for your house and later bring it into the game!

Building Schematics

With the layer system, you can accurately recreate structures like farms or houses, step by step.

Powerful, Yet Simple Features

We've packed MinePaint with tools that are easy to learn but powerful in practice.

Many tools

They make drawing and designing easier. Try the “fill” tool – hold the left mouse button to select an area on the canvas, then fill it with a chosen block or clear it by confirming the selection with the right mouse button.

Sun & Moon Control

Set the mood by placing and positioning the sun or moon, complete with lunar phases.

Save your work

You can save your creation using the “Save” button and return to it later with the “Load” button. You can also save your work as an image by clicking “Save PNG”.
